Frequently Asked Questions

Q. Can I purchase tours straight from kpopstop?
A. No, kpopstop is not a ticketing site for tours. Our goal is to help you find compare the best tours for your kpop experience. Through our filtering system, you can compare tours and choose the best for you, with easy links straight to the tour site for purchase.


Q. What happens if I want to cancel my tour?
A. You will need to get in contact with the site with whom you purchased the tour from. We do not do any type of exchange with the ticketed sites.


Q. How do I know these tour sites are reliable?
A. Our team has spent hours vetting the best tour sites for you. We only offer links to sites that are varified and trustworthy.


Q. How can I get a hold of kpopstop?
A. We encourage everyone to contact us through our Contact page. Leave us a message, whether you just want to say hi, or have any concerns or comments for us!
