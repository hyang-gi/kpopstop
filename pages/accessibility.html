Our Statement on Accessibility

KpopStop is dedicated to creating an inclusive online platform that is enjoyable and accessible for the diverse fandom of K-pop. We follow the WCAG 2.2 guidelines and the European Accessibility Act (EAA) to ensure we have implemented the most up-to-date information and technology for accessibility. We annually review our site's accessibility and policy, as well as, all new legislation, so our users have the best experience when visiting our site! We encourage all who use our site to send us feedback if they experience any accessibility issues so we can continue to create an enjoyable experience for all.
