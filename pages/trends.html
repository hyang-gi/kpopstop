  • Big Hit Entertainment moves into new headquarters in Yongsan

    Big Hit Entertainment moved into its new headquarters building in Yongsan, central Seoul, ahead of the launch of its new identity as entertainment lifestyle platform company HYBE on March 30. The entertainment company announced on Monday that it has officially moved to the new 19-story building, which now houses 1,000 employees from Big Hit Labels — the current name for the affiliates and sub-labels signed to Big Hit Entertainment. Under HYBE, three business units will exist — HYBE Labels, HYBE Solutions and HYBE Platforms. HYBE Labels includes Big Hit Music, Belift Lab, Source Music, Pledis Entertainment, Koz Entertainment and HYBE Labels Japan. Each label will maintain its independence and continue to care for its associated artists as they did before.
